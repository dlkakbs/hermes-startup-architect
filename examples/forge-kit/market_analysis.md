
# Market Analysis: FORGE (The AI Asset Marketplace)
**Date:** February 2026
**Status:** High-Growth Opportunity

## TAM / SAM / SOM (2026 Estimates)
*   **TAM (Total Addressable Market): $1.2 Trillion** 
    Combined value of the global Generative AI sector and SaaS development market as "vibe coding" becomes the primary method for software creation.
*   **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): $14.1 Billion**
    The projected valuation of the specialized AI Prompt and Agentic Workflow marketplace by late 2026/early 2027.
*   **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market): $215 Million**
    Initial 1.5% capture of the Vibe Coding and No-Code asset trade within the first 24 months of FORGE operations.

## Market Trends & Why Now?
1. **The Vibe Coding Revolution:** 75% of new enterprise apps are now built using natural language instead of traditional syntax. Founders need a place to buy "The Vibe" (the prompts/logic) rather than the binary.
2. **Agentic Workflows:** The shift from simple chat responses to autonomous agent chains (n8n, Replit Agents) has created a high-demand for pre-configured, verified logic files.
3. **The Monetization Gap:** While thousands of people are "vibe coding" on social media, there is no centralized, high-trust marketplace that verifies the functionality of these files before purchase.

## Key Opportunities
* **Security & Verification:** Being the first marketplace to "sandbox" and verify AI-generated code snippets.
* **Specialized Categorization:** Focusing on n8n workflows, LangGraph logic, and Tailwind-based vibe-coded landing pages.
