
# Pitch Deck: FORGE

## Slide 1: The Problem
* **The "Vibe Coding" Explosion.** Millions of apps and workflows are being built by "natural language developers" with no way to monetize them.
* **The Trust Gap.** Current marketplaces don't verify if AI-generated code actually works.
* **Fragmentation.** Content is scattered across X, Discord, and personal Gumroads.

## Slide 2: The Solution
* **FORGE Marketplace.** A high-trust ecosystem for selling AI-built assets, workflows, and prompts.
* **Verified Assets.** Every listing is sandboxed and checked by our internal agents.
* **FORGE Connect.** A seamless checkout experience for "one-click" workflow purchases.

## Slide 3: Market Size
* **TAM: $1.2 Trillion.** Global Generative AI and low-code market growth.
* **SAM: $14.1 Billion.** The specialized marketplace for AI assets by 2034.
* **SOM: $215 Million.** Direct initial capture of the Vibe Coding economy.

## Slide 4: Business Model
* **15% Transaction Fee.** A fair cut for high-trust verification and marketplace traffic.
* **FORGE Pro Subscription.** Recurring revenue from high-frequency sellers.
* **B2B Licensing.** Enterprise "In-House" Forge for secure team asset sharing.

## Slide 5: The Ask
* **Seed Funding / Partners.** To scale the "Vibe Verification" compute and build the world's largest repository of agentic logic.
* **The Goal.** Become the "GitHub for the 100 Million Builders" who don't write syntax, but craft "vibes."
