
# Product Roadmap: FORGE (12-Month Execution)

## Month 1-3: MVP & Beta Launch
* **Alpha Build:** Sandbox environment integration and basic marketplace UI.
* **Community Outreach:** Onboard 50 "Vibe Coders" from X/LinkedIn for exclusive early listings.
* **Beta Launch:** Live marketplace with n8n and Prompt Pack support.

## Month 4-6: Feedback & Growth
* **Expansion:** Support for full-stack Replit/Next.js "vibe-coded" micro-SaaS templates.
* **FORGE Pro:** Launch the seller subscription tier with enhanced analytics.
* **Affiliate Engine:** Enable creators to earn by promoting FORGE assets.

## Month 7-12: Scale & Ecosystem
* **FORGE API:** Allow developers to search and "fetch" workflows directly into their own IDEs.
* **Global Hub:** Localized marketplaces and "Enterprise Forge" for secure internal asset stores.
* **The Billion Asset Goal:** Reaching 1M+ verified listings across all AI categories.
