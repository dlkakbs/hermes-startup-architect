
# Business Model: FORGE

## Revenue Streams
1. **Marketplace Commission:** 15% fee on every successful transaction.
2. **FORGE Pro (Subscription):** $29/mo for sellers to get higher search priority, lowered 5% commission, and "Verified Builder" status.
3. **Enterprise Whitelabel:** License the FORGE infrastructure to companies for internal "Agentic Asset Stores."

## Payout Structure
* **Seller Share:** 85% of transaction value.
* **Platform Share:** 15% (Covers hosting, Stripe fees, and verification compute).

## Growth Loops
* **The "Built with FORGE" Watermark:** Every landing page sold includes a tiny badge linking back to the marketplace.
* **Creator Ecosystem:** Incentivizing YouTube/X "Vibe Coders" to list their tutorial assets on FORGE for their audience.

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
* **GMV (Gross Merchandise Volume):** Total volume of assets traded.
* **Verification Pass Rate:** Percentage of assets that successfully clear our AI Sandbox.
* **Seller Retention:** Percentage of builders who list 3+ items.
